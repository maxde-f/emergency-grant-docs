Comprehensive Metrics Framework:

This mind map represents the full spectrum of performance evaluation criteria across PGPX operations:

  • Quality Management (ISO 9001): Risk assessment, incident management, business continuity, supplier quality, process monitoring, continuous improvement
  • Health Systems: Emergency response measures, health program allocation & efficacy, best practices scheduling, contingency planning
  • Project Management: Financial management, budget optimization, stakeholder mapping, LogFrame design, goal setting, risk mitigation
  • Operational Excellence: Strategic planning, policy review, expense categorization, financial reporting
  • Human Resources: Interview scheduling, training and onboarding, capacity building
  • Operations & Admin: Meeting transcription, contact analysis, administrative efficiency
  • Communications: Email campaigns, impact storytelling, content creation, media outreach, data validation
  • Fundraising: Grant reporting, donor communication, grant recovery, capacity-inclusive structuring
  • Feedback Systems: Service delivery monitoring, tutorials, user interaction tracking
  • Data Systems: Data collection & management, compliance verification, research insights
  • Literature & Research: Database research, gap identification, trend analysis, research alignment
